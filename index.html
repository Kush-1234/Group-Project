About Us

Five UNG computer science students collaborating together for a school project founded Group 7 Brewing, one of the fastest growing breweries in north Georgia. Featuring a wide variety of hand crafted beers, G7 offers something for everyone. Our family and dog friendly tap room is a welcoming environment where friends are made while enjoying our delicious craft beers.

Events & Happenings

There's always something brewing at Group 7 Brewing! Join us for:

  • Weekly Trivia Nights

  • Seasonal Brew Releases
  • Community Fundraisers & Game Nights

Food & Kitchen

Pair your pint with a delicious selection from our kitchen!

  • Signature pretzels and house-made beer cheese
  • Smoked brisket sandwiches with locally-sourced ingredients
  • Bavarian sausages, charcuterie boards, and vegan options
  • Seasonal desserts inspired by our house brews
