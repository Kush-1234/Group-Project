About Us

Nestled in the heart of Brewtown, we're a craft brewery
with big love for bold flavors and community.
Founded by like-minded peers, Laika is a space
for good vibes, local shows, and geniune connection.
Whether you're here for a trivia night, a bite, or just to unwind,
you're always welcome at Laika.
