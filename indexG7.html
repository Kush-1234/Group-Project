About Us

Five UNG computer science students collaborating together for a school project founded Group 7 Brewing, one of the fastest growing breweries in north Georgia. Featuring a wide variety of hand crafted beers, G7 offers something for everyone. Our family and dog friendly tap room is a welcoming environment where friends are made while enjoying delicious craft beers.
